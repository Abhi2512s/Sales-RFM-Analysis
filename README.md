# 📊 Sales RFM Analysis Dashboard (Power BI)

## 📌 Project Overview
This project analyzes customer behavior using RFM (Recency, Frequency, Monetary) segmentation.  
The goal is to identify high-value customers, understand purchasing patterns, and support business decision-making.

---

## 🎯 Business Problem
Businesses often struggle to:
- Identify their most valuable customers  
- Understand customer purchasing behavior  
- Improve retention and revenue  

This dashboard solves these problems using data-driven insights.

---

## 🧠 Key Concepts Used
- RFM Analysis (Recency, Frequency, Monetary)
- Customer Segmentation
- Data Cleaning using Power Query
- DAX Measures for calculations

---

## 📊 Dashboard Features

### 1. KPI Cards
- Total Customers  
- Total Revenue  
- Average Frequency  
- Average Monetary Value  

---

### 2. Trend Analysis
- Monthly Revenue and Customer Growth  
- Helps identify business growth patterns  

---

### 3. Customer Segmentation
- Distribution of customers by segment  
- Revenue contribution by segment  

---

### 4. RFM Scatter Plot
- Visualizes customer behavior  
- Identifies active vs inactive customers  

---

## 🔍 Key Insights
- Loyal customers contribute the majority of revenue  
- Regular customers form the largest customer base  
- High-value customers are fewer but highly profitable  
- Significant growth observed after 2011
